## Reference Managers

Organize citations, generate bibliographies, and sync across devices.

| Tool | Price | Platforms | Best For |
|------|-------|-----------|----------|
| [Zotero](https://www.zotero.org/) | Free | Win/Mac/Linux/Web | Open-source, browser integration, group libraries |
| [Mendeley](https://www.mendeley.com/) | Free | Win/Mac/Linux/Web | PDF annotation, social features, Elsevier integration |
| [EndNote](https://endnote.com/) | $274/yr | Win/Mac | Institutional users, advanced features |
| [Paperpile](https://paperpile.com/) | $36/yr | Web/iOS/Android | Google Docs integration, clean UI |
| [RefWorks](https://www.refworks.com/) | Institutional | Web | University libraries, collaboration |
| [Citavi](https://www.citavi.com/) | Free-$119 | Win/Web | Knowledge organization, task planning |

## Discovery & Search

Find relevant papers across databases and disciplines.

- **[Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/)** — The default starting point. Broad coverage, citation tracking.
- **[PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/)** — Biomedical and life sciences. Free access to MEDLINE.
- **[Scopus](https://www.scopus.com/)** — 27,000+ journals. Citation analysis. Requires subscription.
- **[Web of Science](https://www.webofscience.com/)** — High-quality indexed journals. Citation network analysis.
- **[JSTOR](https://www.jstor.org/)** — Humanities and social sciences archives.
- **[IEEE Xplore](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/)** — Engineering and computer science.
- **[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/)** — Free preprints in physics, math, CS, and more.
- **[SSRN](https://www.ssrn.com/)** — Social sciences preprints and working papers.
- **[BASE](https://www.base-search.net/)** — 300M+ documents from 10,000+ sources. Open access focus.
- **[CORE](https://core.ac.uk/)** — Aggregates open access research outputs.

AI-powered tools to help find, organize, and analyze academic literature.

| [ResearchRabbit](https://www.researchrabbit.ai/) | Free | Citation network visualization, paper discovery |
| [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/) | Free | AI-powered academic search, TLDR summaries |
| [Connected Papers](https://www.connectedpapers.com/) | Free tier | Visual paper exploration, find related work |

## Reading & Annotation

Read, highlight, and take notes on PDFs.

- **[Zotero PDF Reader](https://www.zotero.org/support/pdf_reader)** — Built-in reader with annotations synced to your library.
- **[Mendeley](https://www.mendeley.com/)** — Highlight, annotate, and add sticky notes.
- **[MarginNote](https://www.marginnote.com/)** — Turn annotations into mind maps and flashcards. Mac/iOS.
- **[Docear](http://www.docear.org/)** — Mind mapping meets reference management. Open source.
- **[Hypothes.is](https://web.hypothes.is/)** — Annotate any webpage. Collaborative annotations.
- **[Skim](https://skim-app.sourceforge.io/)** — Free PDF reader for Mac with robust annotation.
- **[PDF Expert](https://pdfexpert.com/)** — Clean PDF editing and annotation. Mac/iOS.
- **[Notability](https://notability.com/)** — Handwritten notes on PDFs. iPad-focused.

## Writing & Synthesis

Draft, structure, and polish your literature review.

- **[Scrivener](https://www.literatureandlatte.com/scrivener/)** — Long-form writing with built-in organization. $49.
- **[Overleaf](https://www.overleaf.com/)** — Collaborative LaTeX editing. Free tier available.
- **[Notion](https://www.notion.so/)** — Flexible workspace for notes, databases, and drafts.
- **[Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/)** — Local-first markdown notes with bidirectional linking.
- **[Roam Research](https://roamresearch.com/)** — Networked thought. Daily notes and block references.
- **[Jenni.ai](https://jenni.ai/)** — AI writing assistant for academic writing.
- **[Writefull](https://www.writefull.com/)** — Language feedback trained on academic texts.
- **[Grammarly](https://www.grammarly.com/)** — General writing assistance. Academic tone settings.

## Visualization & Mapping

Visualize citation networks, concept maps, and research landscapes.

- **[Connected Papers](https://www.connectedpapers.com/)** — Visual graph of related papers.
- **[ResearchRabbit](https://www.researchrabbit.ai/)** — Interactive citation network exploration.
- **[VOSviewer](https://www.vosviewer.com/)** — Bibliometric network visualization. Free.
- **[CiteSpace](http://cluster.cis.drexel.edu/~cchen/citespace/)** — Visualize trends and patterns in literature.
- **[Litmaps](https://www.litmaps.com/)** — Discover papers through citation maps.
- **[Inciteful](https://inciteful.xyz/)** — Network analysis of citations. Free.
- **[Open Knowledge Maps](https://openknowledgemaps.org/)** — Visual overview of research topics.

## Systematic Review Tools

Purpose-built for systematic reviews and meta-analyses.

- **[Covidence](https://www.covidence.org/)** — Gold standard for systematic reviews. Screening, extraction, collaboration.
- **[Rayyan](https://www.rayyan.ai/)** — Free systematic review tool. AI-assisted screening.
- **[EPPI-Reviewer](https://eppi.ioe.ac.uk/eppireviewer-web/)** — Comprehensive review software from UCL.
- **[DistillerSR](https://www.distillersr.com/)** — AI-accelerated systematic reviews.
- **[RevMan](https://training.cochrane.org/online-learning/core-software/revman)** — Cochrane's review manager. Meta-analysis tools.
- **[JBI SUMARI](https://sumari.jbi.global/)** — Joanna Briggs Institute review software.
- **[PRISMA](http://www.prisma-statement.org/)** — Not a tool, but the reporting guideline standard.

## Browser Extensions

Capture papers and citations while you browse.

- **[Zotero Connector](https://www.zotero.org/download/connectors)** — Save papers with one click. Auto-captures metadata.
- **[Mendeley Web Importer](https://www.mendeley.com/reference-management/web-importer)** — Import references from any webpage.
- **[Paperpile Extension](https://paperpile.com/chrome/)** — Chrome extension for quick saves.
- **[Unpaywall](https://unpaywall.org/)** — Find free legal versions of paywalled papers.
- **[Google Scholar Button](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/google-scholar-button/)** — Quick access to Scholar search.
- **[Scholarcy](https://www.scholarcy.com/)** — AI summaries of papers in your browser.
